Denouncing Lithuania's False Democracy

The Illusion of Democracy in Lithuania

For almost a century, Lithuania has lived under the lie of a so-called democracy. Ever since Smetona’s 1926 coup, what we've had is nothing more than a rigged system designed to uphold the interests of the elite. Smetona’s authoritarian legacy still runs through our veins today, even though politicians hide behind elections and call it democracy. It’s a false democracy, where the real power stays with those who control the wealth and resources of the country.

The Continued Impact of Smetonian Politics

Since independence, Lithuania has fallen deeper into neoliberal capitalism, and every political party that’s come along has only been a slightly different version of the same pro-capitalist machine. The bourgeoisie controls the show, and politicians—from the conservatives to the so-called centrists—have no solutions for the people. Instead, they keep shoving capitalist policies down our throats, leaving us with wage stagnation, a growing wealth gap, and a workforce forced to emigrate to survive. They’ve perfected the art of distracting us with nationalism and anti-leftist propaganda while ignoring the real problems we face.

"True democracy is not just about elections; it’s about the people having real power."

The Mirage of Choice

Smetonian democracy is still alive, not because of tradition, but because it serves the elite class perfectly. It creates the illusion that we have a choice while keeping us locked in the chains of capitalism. What we’re calling democracy is a scam. The ruling class gets richer, while we—the workers, students, and marginalized—get nothing but more struggle. The system is designed to protect the bourgeoisie, and as long as we stay within its framework, we’ll never see real change.

The Role of the Revolutionary Communist International

This is where the Revolutionary Communist International (RCi) comes in. Unlike the politicians who have sold out to capitalist interests, we’re here to offer the real alternative. The RCi is about restoring true democracy—a democracy where the working class takes control of the economy, the state, and its future. We’re done playing by the rules of the bourgeoisie. It’s time to overthrow this system and establish workers' power.

The Path Forward

We don’t need more parties feeding us the same tired capitalist rhetoric. We need a revolution that destroys the capitalist state and builds a new one in its place—run by and for the people. The current system won’t change unless we make it change. The time for revolutionary action is now.
